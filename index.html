What is "DifferentlyAbled"?

One person in five suffers from a disability. Although strides have already been made in increasing the accessibility of products, services, and technology, there still remains a lot to be accomplished. We hope that through spreading awareness, we will be able to bring positive change to the lives of billions of differently abled people across the globe.
